Vision

Through our experience we know that buying or selling stuff is generally an ordeal for college students, so we have come up with a solution for that!
Students can now easily sell the things they no longer need to others and can also buy useful things with just a few clicks using honeycomb.
Also people can wishlist the products for future reference or request for products they need!

Who we are?

We are a group of Computer Science Undergraduates from IIT Indore.
We have started HoneyComb as a course project with the basic idea to create a website which can solve the most common problems students face during their college life which is buying and selling things.
